Backstory

As a Samsaran, they remember all their past lives, having countless memories and knowledge from hundreds of lives lived, which causes them to be a bit absent-minded. They remember their past more strongly and vividly than the average Samsaran... causing them to confuse which life is their present one, which memories are recent, which name they have this time, and who's friend or foe. Their constant confusion about their current reality causes them to be very trusting and very naive, and they are often wrong about the "facts" and knowledge they have, much of it outdated or simply only true in a past life. They are a simple people, peaceful and easy-going. They know no gender or sexuality, only people and animals alike. They've lived equal lives, animal and human, sometimes unable to tell the difference. They are clumsy, and a bit of a risk taker — not purposefully. Example: They may look off a cliff and need to be stopped from jumping because they just genuinely believe they can fly, as in many of their past lives, they had wings (animals, insects, and winged humanoids), or they might forget they can't breathe underwater due to the memory of being a fish. Mistaking their allergies and what their body needs are just some of the day-to-day confusions they deal with. None of it is a bother to them; it is simply a part of life — their life. Noor's current life is a wandering oracle. They had wonderful parents though — they forgot which ones they were... they've had so many it's difficult to keep track, either way they love them all the same. They heard of the voyage to a new land and found it exciting and a wonderful experience to be had. Noor is a simple person, loving nature and its gifts, enjoying the experience of life as a whole — the good and the bad of it, it's all part of being alive. They're very in tune with their emotions, very slow to anger or sadness, though it's possible. Their reaction to negative emotions is very subdued and mostly internal. They deal with them quietly and on their own.
